ABC Soup Recipe

Vegetables:

  • Tomatoes
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Pearl onions
  • Sweet corn

Meat & Seafood

  • Pork bones
  • Chicken feet
  • Dried scallops

Seasonings

  • Salt
  • Mushroom Powder
  • Black Pepper

Instructions

Prepare Vegetables

  1. Cut all vegetables (except the pearl onions) into bite-sized pieces.

Prepare meat and seafood

  1. Clean pork bones and chicken feet by blanching them in boiling water for 2 minutes
  2. Wash pork bones and chicken feet under cold water.

Time to make Soup!

  1. Place washed meat and seafood in a large pot of water just enough to cover them.
  2. Wait until water starts boiling. Let meat and seafood cook for another 30 minutes.
  3. Add prepped vegetables into pot and let simmer until tender.
  4. Let cook for another hour, add hot water whenever needed.
  5. Season soup with salt, mushroom powder, and black pepper.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
