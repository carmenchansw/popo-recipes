Buddha's Delight Recipe

Aromatics:

  • Set 1

  • Red Fermented Bean Curd
  • Ginger

  • Set 2

  • Garlic
  • Mushrooms
  • Wood Ears
  • Lily buds

Main Ingredients

  • Napa cabbage
  • Fried tofu puffs
  • Mung bean threads

Seasonings

  • Sesame oil
  • Soy sauce

Instructions

Cook aromatics

  1. Heat wok over high heat.
  2. Caramelise ginger for 30 seconds, be careful to not burn it.
  3. Add red fermented bean curd, break it up and cook until fragrant.
  4. Add Set 2 of aromatics and stir fry for 1 minute.

Add main ingredients

  1. Except mung bean noodles, all the main ingredients and stir fry for 2 minutes.

Finish with seasoning

  1. Add seasonings, stir fry everything together for 6 minutes.
  2. Finally, add mung bean noodles, keep stirring until most of the liquid has evaporated.
